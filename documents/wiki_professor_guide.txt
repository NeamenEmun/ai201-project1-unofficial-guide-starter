## The Professors Everyone Wants

**Smith (Data Structures, System Design)**
- Reputation: Rigorous but fair
- Why take him: You'll actually learn. His exams are reasonable if you study his notes.
- Curve: Midterm yes (+5-10), Final no
- Workload: Heavy but not unreasonable (10 hrs/week)
- Verdict: Tough but rewarding. Great for interviews.

**Patel (Web Development, Databases)**
- Reputation: Chill, practical, grades generously
- Why take him: Less stressful, more practical coding
- Curve: Yes (+5-10% typically)
- Workload: Moderate (6-8 hrs/week)
- Verdict: Good GPA course but less deep theory

**Chen (Computer Architecture, Circuits)**
- Reputation: Excellent lecturer, fair grader
- Why take him: Makes dry topics interesting. Projects are hard but educational.
- Curve: Yes, mild (+3-8%)
- Workload: Very heavy on projects (15+ hrs/week)
- Verdict: Demanding but you learn real skills

## The Professors Everyone Should Respect (But Fear)

**Johnson (Algorithms, Advanced Algorithms)**
- Reputation: Hard grader, but rigorous
- Why take her: She teaches you to think. Tech companies notice.
- Curve: Yes (+5-8% typically at end)
- Workload: Heavy and abstract (12+ hrs/week)
- Verdict: Hardest in CS but best for learning

**Wu (Digital Logic, Systems Programming)**
- Reputation: Dense lectures, relentless projects
- Why take him: Foundation course. Must-take. Better hard Wu than bad easy professor.
- Curve: Mild (+5% typically)
- Workload: Very heavy (15+ hrs/week)
- Verdict: Weeder course but essential

**Martinez (Circuits, Electromagnetics)**
- Reputation: Not easy, not mean. Strict.
- Why take him: Most capable professor in engineering. No curve but generous partial credit.
- Curve: No
- Workload: Very heavy (12-15 hrs/week)
- Verdict: Hard but fair. You earn your grade.

## The "Red Flags" Professors

**Gomez (Signals & Systems)**
- Reputation: Genuinely difficult course + difficult professor
- Curve: No
- Pass rate: ~40%
- Workload: Extreme (20+ hrs/week for most)
- Verdict: Only take if you must. Consider waiting for a different professor.

**Rodriguez (Operating Systems)**
- Reputation: Fair but unforgiving. No curve.
- Curve: No
- Projects graded: Strictly on correctness
- Workload: Extreme (15-20 hrs/week)
- Verdict: Essential course but brutal. Plan for suffering.

## The "Legend" Professors (Take Any Course They Offer)

- **Chen** - Makes any topic interesting. Best lecturer.
- **Smith** - Rigorous, fair, teaches real understanding.
- **Patel** - Genuinely cares about student learning + generous grading.

## Choosing Your Schedule

**Hardest first** (recommended): Take Johnson, Wu, Martinez early while you have time/energy. The curves + future coursework is easier.

**Spread it out**: Don't take 3 hard courses same semester. One hard + two moderate works. One hard + two easy is better.

**Professor matters**: A hard course with an excellent professor (Smith, Chen) > an easy course with a bad professor.
